Dear Partner,

The COVID-19 pandemic and its disruption to?colleges and universities may be among the biggest education equity crises in our lifetimes. As we look toward emerging from this crisis, it is important we come together as an educational community to support and learn from each other. On Monday May 11th, we invite educators in higher education throughout California to join a Zoom listening session to share information about the equity challenges that you are experiencing and the emerging bright spots you are seeing related to the pandemic.

Register

All higher education educators are invited, including faculty, counselors/advisors and other student affairs professionals, presidents/executives, and community-based organization professionals involved with two- or four-year institutions.

Organized by The Education Trust–West, and co-sponsored by EdInsights, The Center for Urban Education (CUE), The Center for Research on Educational Access and Leadership (C-REAL), The Community College Equity Assessment Lab (CCEAL), and The California Acceleration Project (CAP), the session’s goal is to hear from educators to ensure that our research and services are responsive to the needs of higher education educators, now and in the coming weeks and months. The session is an opportunity to share in community with other higher education educators about: 1) equity challenges, 2) equity solutions/bright spots, and 3) your thoughts for policy and practice.
